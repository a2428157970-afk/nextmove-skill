# How to Read the Human Career Report

First check the declared mode. Runtime-backed output requires a successful real
offline preflight. Prompt-only output must remain labelled
`NEXTMOVE_PROMPT_ONLY` and is an experience preview.

Read the report in five parts: career profile, evidence-backed strengths, job
fit, transition path, and ordered actions. Verify that every claimed strength
appears in the supplied resume, transferable evidence is not rewritten as
direct target-role experience, and absent information is not treated as a
confirmed capability deficit.

Confidence describes evidence sufficiency, not candidate quality. Match scores,
risks, and recommendations do not guarantee an interview, offer, successful
transition, or other employment outcome. Correct any unsupported statement
before using the report.
