# Prompt-only Output Reading Guide

Every preview must begin with `NEXTMOVE_PROMPT_ONLY`. Treat it as a guided
demonstration of report structure, not as output from the NextMove Runtime.

Check that the report separates source facts, evidence-based interpretation,
and suggestions. Every strength needs source evidence. Transferable capability
is not direct target-role experience, and missing resume evidence is not proof
that a person lacks a capability. Scores and suggestions do not guarantee an
interview, offer, or career outcome.

For runtime-backed results, use the separate Runtime Skill Package and require
its real offline preflight to succeed.
